What is Zeeka?

In simplest words, Zeeka (ℤ) is a cryptocurrency which aims to provide a light and scalable blockchain using the help of Zero-Knowledge proof technology.

Zero-Knowledge proofs? What are they?

Alice wants to prove Bob that she has the key of the door.

What is being proven?

We want to prove a big set of transactions have happened, changing the state of the blockchain from A to B, without showing the transactions.

How does it work?

Through facilitating zero-knowledge proof technology and its advances throughout this decade, a new blockchain infrastracture is introduced, in which batches of state transitions (Transactions) are compressed into constant-sized proofs of validity, and only those proofs are stored on the chain. A novel concept, called Zero Contracts is presented, which is the equivalent of Smart Contracts in some mainstream blockchains (Such as Ethereum), expressed in mathematical constraints, instead of bytecodes of an imaginary virtual machine (E.g. EVM). Integration of those ideas, which were previously used as privacy or layer-2 scaling solutions in other chains, inside the heart of a new blockchain, leads to a new network which is naturally more scalable and has better privacy for its users.
